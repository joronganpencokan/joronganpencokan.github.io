Introduction:
Gmail is one of the most popular email services in the world. Millions of people use it to communicate with their friends, family, and colleagues every day. However, handling a large volume of emails can be a daunting task, especially when we have to open and close each one of them manually. This can lead to a lot of time and effort wasted, which can be used for other activities. In this article, we will show you a secret trick that can help you save time and effort by making Gmail automatically open the next message.

Body:
Step 1: First, open Gmail and click on the gear icon in the top right corner of the page. This will open the settings menu.

Step 2: From the settings menu, select the "Advanced" tab.

Step 3: Scroll down until you see the section called "Auto-advance."

Step 4: Here, you'll see an option to "Enable auto-advance" and two settings: "Go to next conversation" and "Go to previous conversation."

Step 5: Select "Go to next conversation" and click on "Save Changes" at the bottom of the page.

Step 6: Now, whenever you finish reading an email, Gmail will automatically open the next one in your inbox.

Conclusion:
By following the steps above, you can save a lot of time and effort when dealing with a large number of emails in your Gmail inbox. This simple trick can help you focus on important emails and prevent you from wasting time closing and opening each one manually. So, use this trick to streamline your email management and make your life easier. After all, time is a precious commodity, and we should do everything we can to save it.

##   How to Enable and Configure Auto-Advance in Gmail  
 

Here's how to adjust Gmail settings to get Auto-Advance working exactly how you want it to.

 
- Select the Settings gear from your Gmail Inbox.
 - Select See all settings.
 - Select the Advanced tab.
 - Next to Auto-advance, select Enable.
 - Select Save Changes. You're taken back to the Gmail inbox screen.
 - Select Settings > See all settings again. Then, under the General tab, scroll down to the Auto-advance heading.
 - The Auto-advance section only appears under the General tab after you've enabled Auto-advance.
 - You have three options:
 - Go to the next (newer) conversation: When you delete or archive an email, you'll go to the next newest thread.Go to the previous (older) conversation: Instead of the newer message appearing, you'll see the next earliest thread.Go back to the threadlist: You'll return to the inbox. This is effectively the same as turning Auto-advance off.
 - Click the option you want, and then scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Changes.
 - Auto-advance is now on and ready to use.
