# Stop Hackers In Their Tracks: The Foolproof Way To Lock Your Windows 10 PC!

Are you concerned about the security of your Windows 10 PC? You're not alone. With the rise of cybercrime, it's more important than ever to take steps to protect yourself from hackers. Fortunately, there are a number of measures you can take that will make it much more difficult for hackers to break into your PC. In this article, we'll go over some of the best ways to lock your Windows 10 PC to keep your data safe.

## 1. Keep Your Windows Up To Date

One of the most important steps you can take to protect your PC is to keep your Windows 10 operating system up to date. Microsoft regularly releases security updates and patches that address vulnerabilities in the operating system that hackers could exploit. To ensure that your PC is always up to date, go to Settings > Windows Update and make sure that automatic updates are enabled.

## 2. Use A Strong Password

You'd be surprised how many people still use weak, easy-to-guess passwords like "password" or "123456." If your password is too easy to guess, hackers can easily crack it using automated tools. To prevent this, make sure you use a strong password that contains a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Additionally, consider using a password manager like LastPass or Dashlane to generate and store complex passwords for you.

## 3. Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication is a security feature that adds an extra layer of protection to your login process. With two-factor authentication, you'll need to enter a code that's sent to your phone or email in order to log in to your PC. This makes it much more difficult for hackers to gain access to your PC, even if they've somehow managed to find out your password. To enable two-factor authentication in Windows 10, go to Settings > Accounts > Sign-in options.

## 4. Install Antivirus Software

Even if you're careful about the websites you visit and the files you download, it's still possible for malware to find its way onto your PC. To guard against this, you should install a reputable antivirus program like Avast, Norton, or Bitdefender. These programs will scan your PC for viruses and malware and remove any threats that they find.

## 5. Use A Firewall

A firewall is a security barrier that sits between your PC and the internet, blocking incoming traffic that could be hazardous. Windows 10 comes with a built-in firewall that you can configure to your specifications. However, you should consider using a third-party firewall like ZoneAlarm or Comodo Firewall for added security.

## 6. Be Careful About The Sites You Visit And The Files You Download

Finally, it's important to be cautious about the websites you visit and the files you download. Many hackers use phishing attacks to trick users into giving up their passwords or personal information. To avoid falling prey to these attacks, don't click on links in emails from unknown sources, and never download files from untrusted websites.

By following these six tips, you can significantly improve the security of your Windows 10 PC and make it much more difficult for hackers to gain access to your data. As always, it's important to be vigilant and stay informed about the latest threats and security best practices. Stay safe out there!

##   How to Lock Windows 10 PC  
 
###   Use the Windows Lock Key  
 

Perhaps the single easiest and most reliable way to lock your computer is to press Win+L. The computer will lock immediately, and you can enter your passcode when you come back to use it again.

 
###   Use Control+Alt+Delete as a Windows Lock Shortcut  
 

This keyboard shortcut is among the oldest ever created that’s still in daily use by millions of computer users. To lock your computer, press Ctrl+Alt+Delete, then, on the options screen, select Lock.

 
###   Use the Start Menu to Lock the Screen  
 

Windows generally gives you several ways to accomplish a common task, and this is a good example of a less common (and frankly more cumbersome) way to lock the screen.

 
- Select the Start menu.
 - Select the user avatar for your Windows account on the left side of the screen.
 - Select Lock.

##   Use Your Phone with Dynamic Lock to Lock Windows 10  
 

Dynamic Lock is a feature from the Windows 10 Creators Update, released in April 2019. If you pair your smartphone with your computer, you can set your computer to automatically lock when you take your phone out of Bluetooth range.
