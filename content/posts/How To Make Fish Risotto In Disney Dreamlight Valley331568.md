Ingredients

To get started, you'll need to gather the following ingredients:

- 1 cup Arborio rice
- 3 tablespoons of olive oil
- 1 sliced onion
- 3 finely chopped garlic cloves
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups fish stock
- 1/2 cup dry white wine
- 1/2 lb of mussels
- 1/2 lb of scallops
- 1/2 lb of shrimp
- 1/2 cup of frozen peas
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add olive oil. Sauté the onion and garlic until golden brown.

2. Add the Arborio rice to the pan and continuously stir until it’s cooked.

3. Pour in the white wine and cook until the liquid is absorbed.

4. Add the fish stock, a cup at a time, and cook and stir until it’s absorbed each time.

5. Keep stirring the rice while adding the chopped seafood, peas, and salt and pepper to taste. Continue cooking until the seafood is cooked through and the rice is creamy.

6. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese until everything is well combined.

7. Serve the fish risotto hot and garnish with some chopped parsley or lemon wedges for a finishing touch.

## How to Make Fish Risotto in Disney Dreamlight Valley
 

 
Fish Risotto is a classic Italian dish you can make in Dreamlight Valley. Every dish in this game has a Star Rating. The number of Stars tells you how many ingredients are required. It is a three-star dish, meaning you require three ingredients to prepare it.
 
You will need:
 
- Fish
 - Rice
 - Butter

 
### Fish
 
To acquire a fish, you need to go fishing. You will get a Fishing Rod from Goofy after completing the ‘Fishing Expedition’ quest. You can use any fish in this dish and luckily there is plenty of fish to catch throughout the biomes. Like Herring at Dazzle Beach and Glade of Trust, Tuna at Forgotten Lands and Glade of Trust, etc. Note that you cannot use Seafood instead of Fish. These two are different ingredients. You can also get Fish for Risotto in Dreamlight Valley from Moana’s Boat.
 
### Rice
 
To get rice, go to Goofy’s Stall in Glade of Trust. Repair his stall and you can buy Rice Seeds from him for 35 Star Coins. Plant these seeds in rich soil and wait for 50 minutes to grow rice.
 
### Butter
 
Butter is readily available at Chez Remy’s Restaurant. You can buy butter from his back shelf for 190 Star Coins.
 
After gathering all the ingredients, head over to the cooking stove and toss in all the ingredients. Your delicious Fish Risotto will be ready which you can either consume for energy or sell for Star Coins.
