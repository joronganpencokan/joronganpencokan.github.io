So, How do They Cook their Fish?

The cooking method used in Disney Dreamlight Valley is simple and straightforward. However, it's the ingredients and the way they are combined that make this fish a standout dish. Here's the recipe to get you started:

Ingredients:

- 4 medium-sized fish fillets (preferably tilapia, bass or cod)
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 1 tablespoon of butter
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ¼ teaspoon of smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
- 2 tablespoons of fresh parsley, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons of fresh lime juice

Instructions:

1. Rinse the fish fillets and pat them dry with a paper towel. Sprinkle salt and pepper on the fillets, on both sides.

2. Heat a skillet or a griddle over medium heat. Add the olive oil and butter and let it melt.

3. Once the butter and oil mixture is hot, add the minced garlic to it and let it cook until fragrant, for about 1 minute.

4. Add the seasoned fish fillets to the pan, making sure not to overcrowd it.

5. Sprinkle the smoked paprika and oregano over the fillets, and let the fish cook till it's golden brown around the edges, for roughly 3-4 minutes.

6. Flip the fish fillets over gently and let them cook for another 3-4 minutes.

7. While the fish is cooking on the second side, sprinkle fresh parsley on top of the fish.

8. Finally, squeeze some fresh lime juice over the fish fillets and remove them from the heat.

9. Allow the fish to rest for a minute or two to absorb the flavors.

10. Your mouthwatering savory fish is ready to serve.
