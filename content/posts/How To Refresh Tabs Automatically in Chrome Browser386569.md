If you are using the Chrome browser, this hack is perfect for you. By following the steps below, you'll never miss a beat again.

Step 1: Open Chrome and type the following command into the address bar.

chrome://flags/

Step 2: In the search bar, type in "Automatic Tab Discarding" and click on the "Enable Automatic Tab Discarding" option.

Step 3: Once enabled, restart your browser to activate the changes.

That's it! You're all done. Now you can browse the web for as long as you like without having to worry about constantly refreshing your web pages. Chrome will automatically discard inactive tabs, allowing you to keep your current tab active while saving system resources by discarding inactive tabs in the background.

This feature is especially useful for people who have a habit of keeping too many tabs open, which can slow down their computer and make it difficult to find what they are looking for. The Automatic Tab Discarding feature ensures that your browsing experience is faster and more efficient, allowing you to focus on what's important.
