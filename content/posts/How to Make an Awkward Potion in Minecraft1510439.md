##   How to Make an Awkward Potion in Minecraft  
 

Follow these steps to collect the materials and craft an Awkward Potion in Minecraft.

 
- Craft Blaze Powder with a Blaze Rod.
 - Make a Crafting Table with 4 wood planks. Any type of plank (Warped Planks, Crimson Planks, etc.) will do.
 - Place the Crafting Table on the ground and open it to bring up the 3X3 crafting grid.
 - Craft a Brewing Stand. Put 1 Blaze Rod in the middle of the top row, then put 3 Cobblestones in the middle row.
 - Place the Brewing Stand on the ground and interact with it to bring up the brewing menu.
 - Add 1 Blaze Powder to the box in the upper-left corner of the brewing menu to activate the Brewing Stand.
 - Put a Water Bottle in one of the three boxes at the bottom of the brewing menu.
 - Make up-to three potions at once by putting Water Bottles in the other bottom boxes.
 - Put 1 Nether Wart in the box at the top of the brewing menu.
 - Wait for the brewing process to complete. When the progress bar is full, your bottle will contain an Awkward Potion.
