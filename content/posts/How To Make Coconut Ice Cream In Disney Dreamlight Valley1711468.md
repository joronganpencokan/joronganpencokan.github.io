Are you looking for a refreshing and delicious treat to cool you down during your Disney Dreamlight Valley experience? Look no further because homemade coconut ice cream is just what you need! You won't believe how simple it is to make this tasty frozen dessert, and we have the recipe right here!

Ingredients:

- 1 can of coconut milk
- 1 cup of heavy cream
- 1/2 cup of sugar
- 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
- A pinch of salt

Instructions:

1) In a large bowl, whisk together the coconut milk, heavy cream, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined.

2) Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.

3) Once the ice cream is churned, transfer it to a freezer-safe container and freeze until firm, about 4 hours or overnight.

4) Serve and enjoy!

It really is as simple as that! The combination of creamy coconut milk and rich heavy cream makes for a decadent and indulgent ice cream that's perfect for any sunny day in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

## How to Make Coconut Ice Cream in Disney Dreamlight Valley
 

 
Coconut Ice Cream is a dessert you can make in this game. It is a four-star dish meaning you will require four ingredients to prepare the dish. You can find two of the ingredients in Chez Remy’s kitchen itself for this recipe.
 
### Slush Ice
 
You can get Slush Ice from Chez Remy’s Back Shelf once you have unlocked it. Reach Friendship Level 10 with Remy to unlock Slush Ice. You can purchase it for 150 Star Coins.
 
### Coconut
 
Coconut will be available to you in Moana’s realm. You can harvest it from trees or complete Moana and Maui’s quest to unlock coconut trees at Dazzle Beach. Coconuts are essential for more recipes like Coconut Ice Cream in Disney Dreamlight Valley so make sure to unlock them.
 
### Sugarcane
 
To get Sugarcane, you will have to go to Goofy’s stall at Dazzle Beach. Sugarcane seeds will be available after you repair the stall. You can also buy Sugarcane itself from Goofy by upgrading his stall. The seeds cost you 5 Star Coins while the Sugarcane costs you 29 Star Coins.
 
### Milk
 
Milk is available on Chez Remy’s Back shelf. You can buy it for 230 Star Coins.
 
To make Coconut Ice Cream, simply toss all the ingredients in the boiling pot of water. Now it might seem strange to make Ice Cream on a stove but that’s how you make it.
