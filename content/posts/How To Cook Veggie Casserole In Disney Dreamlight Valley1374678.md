Ingredients:
- 1 pound of spinach
- 2 medium red bell peppers, chopped
- 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 can of diced tomatoes
- 1/2 cup of vegetable broth
- 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon of smoked paprika
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F.
2. In a large skillet, sauté the red bell peppers, onion, and garlic until tender.
3. Add the spinach to the skillet and cook until wilted.
4. Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the skillet, and stir well.
5. Transfer the mixture to a casserole dish and spread it out evenly.
6. Top the veggie mixture with grated Parmesan cheese, if using.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

This Veggie Casserole is not only delicious, but it is also packed with nutrients and fiber. Spinach is an excellent source of iron, vitamin K, and calcium, while chickpeas are a great source of protein and fiber. Red bell peppers are rich in vitamin C, and onions and garlic have immune-boosting properties. This dish is perfect for a healthy and hearty dinner.
