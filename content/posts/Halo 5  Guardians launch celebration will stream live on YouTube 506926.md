# You Won’t Want to Miss This! Halo 5 Guardians Launch Celebration Goes LIVE on YouTube!

Get ready to celebrate, gamers! The launch of Halo 5: Guardians is almost upon us and Microsoft is gearing up for a spectacular celebration.

This year, the Halo 5: Guardians Launch Celebration goes LIVE on YouTube, making it a global event accessible to all fans. The celebration will feature a host of exclusive content, interviews with the development team and special guests, all set against the backdrop of the iconic UNSC Infinity.

Fans from all around the world can tune in to watch the broadcast and join the celebration. The event promises to be an unforgettable experience, packed with exciting new reveals, behind-the scenes insights and more.

The Halo franchise has been a gaming icon for more than a decade, and Halo 5: Guardians is set to be the most ambitious and epic game in the series to date. Set in a war-torn galaxy, players step into the boots of Spartan Locke and master Chief as they lead their respective teams on a perilous journey to save the galaxy from an imminent threat.

The game boasts stunning next-gen graphics, epic multiplayer battles, and a host of exciting new features that are sure to keep players engaged for hours on end. With its mix of classic Halo gameplay and new innovations, Halo 5: Guardians is set to be the must-have game of this year.

For fans of the franchise, the Halo 5: Guardians Launch Celebration is an event not to be missed. This is your chance to get an exclusive look at the game before it hits the shelves, and to hear from the developers themselves about the inspiration and development behind the game.

So mark your calendars for October 26th, and get ready to be transported into the world of Halo. The celebration starts at 6 pm PT/9 pm ET on YouTube, so be sure to tune in and join the party. With its stunning graphics, epic multiplayer battles, and exciting new features, Halo 5: Guardians is the game that gamers have been waiting for. And with the Halo 5: Guardians Launch Celebration going LIVE on YouTube, this is an event you won’t want to miss!

As the global “Halo 5: Guardians” launch approaches very fast, Microsoft announces that will be streaming the celebration live exclusively in YouTube. The “Halo 5: Live” event will begin on October 26th, at 6 PM EST until midnight when the game goes live in United States.
 
During the broadcast, you will be able to see exclusive gameplay, celebrity appearances, live interviews with the team of 343 Industries.
 
“Halo 5: Guardians is the most ambitious addition to the $4.6 billion sci-fi franchise to date, bringing together a gripping story, deep character narrative and teamwork-based gameplay.” Microsoft explains.
 
“‘Halo 5: Guardians’ is the game we have always dreamed of making, and it was important to us that Halo fans in every corner of the world could join us in an epic-scale launch celebration,” said Bonnie Ross, corporate vice president and head of 343 Industries.
 
The global launch event will also include the world premiere of “Halo: The Fall of Reach” episode one, which is a new animated series that tell the origin behind the SPARTAN program.
 
The first Xbox roundtable featuring the writers behind “Halo 5: Guardians” and Nathan Fillion, the voice of Spartan Buck.
 
And there will different “Halo 5” celebrations in important cities around the world, including at the new Microsoft at Fifth Ave. and 53rd St., located in the heart of Manhattan. A lucky fan in Australia will play “Halo 5: Guardians” from a helicopter flying over Sydney, as gameplay is broadcasted via a screen suspended from a second helicopter. In London, iconic entertainment venue The Roundhouse will transform into a “Halo 5: Guardians” celebration, and much more.
 
In all Microsoft stores, the company will be streaming “Halo 5: Live” and they will be offering an exclusive Halo poster for $25, early gameplay, and more until the celebration culminates at midnight.
