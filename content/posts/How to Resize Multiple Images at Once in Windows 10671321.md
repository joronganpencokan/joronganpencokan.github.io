Discover the Time-Saving Hack to Resize Dozens of Images in Windows 10 with Just One Click!

If you're someone who works with images regularly, you know how tedious and time-consuming it can be to resize multiple images one by one. It can take several hours, and in some cases even days, to manually resize dozens of images. Thankfully, there is a time-saving hack to resize dozens of images in Windows 10 with just one click!

Windows 10 has a built-in batch image resizing tool that will save you hours of time. You can resize multiple images quickly, easily, and in just a few clicks. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Select the Images

The first step is to select the images you want to resize. To do this, navigate to the images you want to resize and select them all by holding down the Shift key and clicking on the first and last images in the group. Alternatively, you can hold down the Ctrl key and click on each image that you want to resize.

Step 2: Resize the Images

Once you have selected the images, right-click on any one of them and select 'Resize Pictures' from the drop-down menu. This will open the Resize Pictures dialog box.

In the Resize Pictures dialog box, you can choose the desired size for your images. Windows 10 offers four options for resizing images: Small (640 x 480), Medium (800 x 600), Large (1024 x 768), and Email (1600 x 1200). Choose the size that best suits your needs.

Step 3: Save the Resized Images

After you have chosen the desired image size, click on the 'OK' button in the Resize Pictures dialog box. This will resize all the selected images to the chosen size. The resized images will be saved in the same folder as the original images.

That's it! You have just saved yourself hours of time resizing multiple images. Now you can focus on other important tasks or enjoy some leisure time with your family and friends.
