If you own a Samsung phone, you have access to some of the most advanced technology on the market. However, it's all for naught if your device isn't optimized to its fullest potential. That's where the installation of a must-have widget comes in. 

Widgets are one of the essential features of the Android operating system that enable you to access quick information, control your device, and help keep you connected and informed at all times. One of the most useful widgets available for Samsung phones is called the Edge Panel. 

This widget is so named because it is an edge-screen feature that is specific to Samsung phones. The Edge Panel is a customizable navigation tool that provides quick access to your most frequently used apps, contacts, and other valuable content. You can easily slide it out from the right edge of your phone to access its many features.

Some of the top features of the Edge Panel include the ability to access your most-used apps, such as your email, messaging, and calendar apps, with ease. You can also use it to access shortcuts to frequently called contacts, making it a great tool to have when you need to get in touch with someone quickly.

One of the most significant benefits of the Edge Panel widget is that you can customize it to fit your particular needs. You can even download new Edge Panels from the Samsung Store that offer even more customization options. 

To download the Edge Panel widget, go to Settings > Display > Edge Screen > Edge Panels, and then select the Edge Panel you want to customize. You can customize its size, function, and appearance to suit your needs. 

Having the Edge Panel widget on your Samsung phone provides quick and effortless access to features that can save time and make your device more efficient. It's an excellent tool to have, whether you're a busy professional, a student, or anyone who needs to stay on top of things throughout the day.

Unleash the full potential of your Samsung phone by installing this must-have widget today, and see how much easier it makes your life. With the Edge Panel widget, you'll have everything you need at your fingertips, making your device even more useful and convenient.

##   How to Add Widgets on a Samsung Phone or Tablet  
 

Widgets allow you to personalize what displays on your screen so that you're only getting the information you need at a glance. Many specific widgets, like 1Weather or Calendar, are available in the Play Store as standalone apps. Some are available in different sizes, letting you decide how much of the screen you want them to fill.

 

To add a calendar widget that counts down to an important event:

 
- Touch and hold on the home screen, then tap Widgets at the bottom of the screen.
 - Select Calendar.
 - Touch and hold Countdown.
 - Drag-and-drop the widget where you want it to appear on your screen.
 - Select the event in your calendar that you want to count down to.
 - Choose the Background color and Background transparency, then select the left arrow (<) next to Widget settings in the top-left corner.
 - You can rearrange widgets by touching and dragging them around the screen. To remove widgets, touch and hold the widget you want to delete, then choose remove.
