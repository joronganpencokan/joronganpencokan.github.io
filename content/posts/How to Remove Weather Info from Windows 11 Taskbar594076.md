Say Goodbye to Annoying Weather Updates on Windows 11 Taskbar with this One Simple Trick!

Are you annoyed with the constant weather updates that appear on your Windows 11 taskbar? We understand that these notifications can be distracting and take up valuable space on your screen. But what if we told you there's a simple solution to this problem?

Introducing the "News and Interests" feature on your Windows 11 taskbar. This nifty feature allows you to customize the information that appears on your taskbar, including the weather updates. With just a few clicks, you can say goodbye to those annoying weather notifications forever.

To access the "News and Interests" feature, simply right-click on an empty space on your taskbar and select "News and Interests". Alternatively, you can also click the icon on your taskbar that looks like a small weather icon.

Once you've opened the "News and Interests" panel, you'll see a variety of customization options available to you. Here, you can choose which types of news stories and weather updates you want to see. You can also change the location that your weather updates are based on.

To turn off the weather updates completely, simply click on the gear icon in the "News and Interests" panel. This will take you to the settings menu where you can uncheck the "Show weather updates" box.

By customizing your "News and Interests" on your Windows 11 taskbar, you're able to keep your screen space clear and only receive the information that's important to you. Say goodbye to those annoying weather notifications and hello to a cleaner, more personalized taskbar experience.

Along with the Start Menu, new wallpapers, new icons, you might have also noticed the weather widget that appears on the taskbar. By default, the weather widget appears on the left side of the taskbar, but if you change the taskbar icon alignment to the left, the Weather widget will show up right next to the Start button.
Unlike Windows 10, you can’t move the Weather widget to the right side of the Taskbar on Windows 11. On Windows 11, the weather widget serves two purposes – It shows you the weather information of your location and brings up the Widget panel when you click or hover your mouse cursor over it

So, the Weather icon on the Windows 11 taskbar is actually a widget that’s configured to show you the weather information of your selected region. Although the Weather widget could be a useful option for some, it’s not meant for everyone.

 
## Steps to Remove Weather Info from Windows 11 Taskbar


The weather widget runs in the background to update the weather condition and consumes your RAM and CPU resources. Also, the weather information that Microsoft shows is not accurate; hence, you can disable it without any second thought.
So, if you don’t want to see the weather information on the Windows 11 taskbar, you need to hide the Weather widget manually. Hence, in this article, we will share a step-by-step guide on how to remove weather from Windows 11 taskbar. Let’s check out.
1. First of all, click on the Windows 11 Start button and select Settings.

2. On the Settings app, click on the Personalization option, as shown in the screenshot below.

3. On the right, click on the Taskbar option.

4. On the Taskbar page, click on the Taskbar items. On the Taskbar items, you need to turn off the Widgets option.

That’s it! You are done. This will immediately remove the Weather widget from your Windows 11’s taskbar.
Although the weather widget on Windows 11 serves as a handy option to check weather conditions, it consumes space and cluttering the taskbar.
