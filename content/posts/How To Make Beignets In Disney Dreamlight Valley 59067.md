Below is the recipe that will guide you through the making of the mouthwatering beignets:

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups warm water
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 envelope active dry yeast
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 1/4 teaspoons salt
- 1 cup evaporated milk
- 7 cups bread flour
- 1/4 cup shortening
- Vegetable oil for frying
- Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions:

1. In a large bowl, add warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 10 minutes or until the yeast is activated.

2. Add the beaten eggs, salt, and evaporated milk to the yeast mixture and stir until combined.

3. In a separate bowl, sift the bread flour and add it to the wet mixture, 1 cup at a time, while mixing until smooth. Once all the flour is added, add the shortening, mix well, and knead the dough for a few minutes.

4. Cover the dough and let it sit in a warm place for at least 2 hours until the dough has doubled in size.

5. Preheat the vegetable oil in a deep fryer or a heavy-duty pot to 365°F.

6. Roll the dough on a floured surface to 1/4-inch thickness and cut the dough into small rounds.

7. Carefully add the rounds into the hot oil and fry until golden brown, turning once.

8. Place the fried beignets on a paper towel-lined plate to drain the excess oil.

9. Dust the beignets with powdered sugar, and serve warm.

## How to Make Beignets in Disney Dreamlight Valley
 

 
Beignets are deep-fried pastries covered with powdered sugar and you can make them in this game. Beignet is a four-star recipe, meaning you will need four ingredients to prepare this sugary dessert.
 
### Canola
 
You can buy Canola Seeds at Goofy’s stall in the Forest of Valor. They are the most sought-after vegetables because of their high selling price. It costs 25 Star Coins to buy Canola Seeds. You can also obtain them by removing Night Thorns for free.
 
### Sugarcane
 
Sugarcane can be either grown or purchased in this game. You can buy Sugarcane Seeds at Goofy’s stall at Dazzle Beach. You will have to repair Goofy’s stall to purchase seeds and upgrade it to buy Sugarcane. The price of Sugarcane Seeds is 5 Star Coins while the price of Sugarcane is 29 Star Coins. As Beignets are a dessert dish in Disney Dreamlight Valley, Sugarcane is a must.
 
### Wheat
 
Wheat Seeds are the cheapest seeds you will find in the game. You can buy them from Goofy’s stall at Peaceful Meadow. You will have to repair it first to get them. Plant those seeds and wait for a minute to get Wheat.
 
### Eggs
 
Eggs are easily available at Chez Remy’s Back Shelf. You can buy them for 220 Star Coins.
 
Once you’ve gathered all the ingredients, head over to the stove and start cooking. Toss all the ingredients in the cooking pot and you will get your Beignets.
