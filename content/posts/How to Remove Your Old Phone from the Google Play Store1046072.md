Say Goodbye to Your Old Phone Forever - Learn How to Remove it from Google Play Store NOW!

With the constant release of new smartphones and technology advancements, upgrading to a new phone has become a common trend. However, many people forget to properly dispose of their old phones, leaving them vulnerable to privacy breaches and cyber-attacks. If you're one of them, it's time to learn how to remove your old phone from the Google Play Store and wave it goodbye forever.

Taking this step not only ensures your privacy but also prevents any unauthorized access to your personal information. For instance, if you still have your old phone in your Google Play Store account, hackers can easily access your device, install malicious software, and use it to extract personal data like contents of emails and text messages, contacts, videos, and photos.

Therefore, removing your old phone from Google Play Store is essential, and it doesn't take much time. In this article, we'll guide you on how to do just that.

Step 1: Access Your Google Account

The first step involves accessing your Google account on your computer or device. Once you're logged in, navigate to the 'Google Play Store' tab.

Step 2: Click on the Menu Bar

Located on the top left corner of the screen, the menu bar consists of various options such as 'my apps and games,' 'settings,' and 'play protect.' Click on it to reveal the different options.

Step 3: Click on 'My Devices' Option

Among the options on the menu bar, click on 'my devices' to reveal the list of devices linked to your Google Play Store account. Here, you'll find your old phone.

Step 4: Click 'Settings' Button

Once you've located your old phone, click on the 'settings' button next to it.

Step 5: Click on 'Unregister'

The last step in removing your old phone from Google Play Store is clicking on the 'Unregister' button, located at the bottom of the screen. This will prompt a confirmation message popup. Click on 'Ok' to complete the process.

Once done, your old phone won't be visible on your Google account. Moreover, any app updates or installations will be removed from your old phone, preventing unauthorized access and cyber-attacks.

In conclusion, upgrading to a new phone is exciting, but don't forget to dispose of your phone properly. Removing your old phone from Google Play Store is a quick and easy process that guarantees your privacy and security. Follow these five simple steps, and say goodbye to your old phone forever.

## Steps to Remove Your Old Phone from the Google Play Store


So, in this article, we will share a step-by-step guide on how to remove your old phone from Google Play Store. Let’s check out.
1. First of all, open the web browser on your PC. Next, log in with your Google Account.
2. Now, open the Google Play Store in your web browser.

3. Now, click on the small gear icon displayed on the top-right of your screen.

4. From the list of options, click on Settings.
5. On the Settings page, scroll down to the My Devices option. You will find your devices here.

6. To hide the device, you need to uncheck the ‘Show in menus’ box in the ‘Visibility’ column.


 
### How to Rename Android Devices on the Google Play Store?


Well, if you want to change your Android device name on the Google Play Store, you need to click on the ‘Edit‘ button behind the device you wish to rename.

Now you need to enter the new name in the box under Nickname. Once done, click on the Update button.

That’s it! You are done. This will rename your Android device on the Google Play Store.
