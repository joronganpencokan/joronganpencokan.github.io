## Ingredients

Before we get to the recipe, let's take a look at the ingredients you'll need to make this delicious dish.

- 1 cup of cooked white rice
- 4 ounces of thinly sliced beef
- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
- 1 clove of garlic, minced
- 1/2 onion, chopped
- 1/2 carrot, julienned
- 1/2 red bell pepper, julienned
- 1/2 green bell pepper, julienned
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Directions

Now that you have all the ingredients ready, it's time to start cooking!

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.

3. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned.

4. Add the chopped onion and cook until translucent.

5. Add the julienned carrot, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper, and sauté until tender.

6. Add the cooked white rice to the skillet and stir to combine.

7. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce and brown sugar until well combined.

8. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the rice and stir to coat.

9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

10. Serve hot and enjoy!
