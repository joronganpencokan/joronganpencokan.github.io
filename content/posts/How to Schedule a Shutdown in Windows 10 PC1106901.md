# You Won't Believe How Easy It is to Schedule Automatic Shutdown on Your Windows 10 PC – Here's How!

Have you ever wanted to turn off your PC automatically after a certain period of time? Perhaps you fell asleep while streaming a movie, and you woke up later with your computer still running. Worry no more because scheduling automatic shutdown on your Windows 10 PC is easier than you think. 

Windows 10 has a built-in tool that lets you schedule a specific time or period for your PC to turn off, and it's incredibly easy to set up. Here's how you can do it:

## Step 1: Open the Task Scheduler

To open the task scheduler, you can either search for it in the start menu or by pressing the Windows key + R to open the Run window, then type "taskschd.msc," and hit Enter. 

## Step 2: Create a New Task

In the Task Scheduler window, click on "Create Task" on the right-hand side of the screen. 

## Step 3: Name the Task

In the General tab of the Create Task window, give your task a name that you will easily remember. 

## Step 4: Set a Time Trigger

In the Triggers tab, click "New" and select when you want your PC to turn off. You can choose to have it turn off daily, weekly, or monthly at a specific time. 

## Step 5: Choose an Action

In the Actions tab, click "New" and select "Shutdown" from the drop-down list. 

## Step 6: Save and Exit

After you have completed all the necessary steps, click "OK" to save your task. Your PC will now turn off automatically at your selected time. 

## Additional Tips

- If you want to cancel your scheduled shutdown, go back to the Task Scheduler window and delete the task you created. 
- You can also create multiple tasks if you want your PC to turn off at different times depending on your schedule.
- If you experience any issues when trying to set up automatic shutdown on Windows 10, check that you have administrator rights on your PC, and try again. 

In conclusion, scheduling automatic shutdown on your Windows 10 PC is incredibly easy and takes only a few minutes. It's a great way to save energy and prolong the life of your PC by avoiding overheating or excessive usage. So, try it out today, and enjoy the benefits of having an automatically managed PC.

## Schedule a Shutdown in Windows 10 PC


Hence in this article, we will show you the best method to schedule an automatic shutdown and startup on Windows 10 operating system.
The process is a bit complicated, so follow it carefully. Let’s check out.
1. First of all, head to the Control Panel > Administrator Tools > Task Scheduler.
2. Under the Task Scheduler option, click on ‘Create Basic Task’ and name it something like ‘Shutdown.’

3. After naming the new task, click on the Next button.

4. In the next step, select ‘Trigger‘ and then click on ‘Daily‘. Now, you need to enter the time and date you want your computer to shut down.

5. Click on ‘Next’ and then select Start a Program. Now under the ‘Program/Script’ enter ‘Shutdown‘. On the ‘Add Arguments’ box, enter -s -f -t 60

Important: The ’60’ in the script represents the seconds you have until the countdown ends. If you want to make it an immediate shutdown, change that argument to t-0
That’s it! You are done. This is how you can schedule Windows 10 shutdown.
