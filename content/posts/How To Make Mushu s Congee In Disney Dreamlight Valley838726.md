Congee, also known as rice porridge, is a traditional Chinese dish that has been around for centuries. It is made by cooking rice in an excessive amount of water until it breaks down to a thick and creamy texture. The best part about congee is that it is highly customizable, meaning you can add in any meat, seafood, or vegetable of your choice to make it your own. To make Mushu's famous congee, you'll need the following ingredients:

• 1 cup of long-grain rice
• 10 cups of water
• 1 1/2 tablespoon of salt
• 1 tablespoon of ginger, minced
• 1 tablespoon of garlic, minced
• 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
• 1/4 cup of chopped green onion
• 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
• 2 cups of cooked and shredded chicken

Instructions:

1. Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
2. In a large pot, add the rice, water, salt, garlic, and ginger. Bring the pot to a boil.
3. Lower the heat to medium-low and let it simmer for an hour, stirring occasionally.
4. Once the rice has broken down into a thick and creamy texture, add in the soy sauce, green onion, cilantro, and shredded chicken.
5. Stir until everything is well combined.
6. Serve hot and enjoy.
