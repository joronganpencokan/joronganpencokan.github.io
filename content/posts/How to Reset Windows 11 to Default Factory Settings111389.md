What is a Windows 11 Reset?

A Windows 11 reset is a feature that allows you to reinstall Windows while keeping your files, personal settings, and apps. It restores your system to its original state, just like it was when you first purchased it. A reset can resolve many issues, such as performance problems, app crashes, and system errors. It's a great way to start fresh without losing your data.

How to Reset Windows 11

To reset Windows 11, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Press the Windows key + I to open Settings.

Step 2: Click on Update & Security.

Step 3: Click on Recovery.

Step 4: Click on Get started under Reset this PC.

Step 5: Choose whether you want to keep your files or remove everything.

Step 6: Select the appropriate option to carry out the reset.

The reset process may take a while to complete, depending on the size of your hard drive and the number of files you have. Once it's done, your system will be restored to its original state.
