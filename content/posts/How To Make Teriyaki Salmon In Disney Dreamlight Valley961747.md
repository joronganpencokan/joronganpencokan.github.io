Ingredients:

- 2 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
- 2 tbsp. teriyaki sauce
- 2 tbsp. honey
- 2 tbsp. soy sauce
- 1 garlic clove (minced)
- 1 tbsp. grated ginger
- 1 tbsp. sesame oil
- Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 400°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. In a small bowl, mix together teriyaki sauce, honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes (if using).

3. Place the salmon fillets on the baking sheet, skin side down, and season with salt and pepper.

4. Brush the teriyaki sauce mixture over the salmon fillets, making sure they are evenly coated. Reserve half of the mixture for later.

5. Bake the salmon for 10-12 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F.

6. Remove the salmon from the oven and brush the remaining teriyaki sauce mixture over the fillets.

7. Serve the salmon hot with a side of steamed vegetables, rice or both.

## How to Make Teriyaki Salmon in Disney Dreamlight Valley
 

 
Teriyaki Salmon is an advanced dish that you can prepare in Dreamlight Valley. It’s a five-star dish so you will require five ingredients to make this dish. You will find these ingredients in different areas of the game. You will also have to unlock Chez Remy to cook this dish. The ingredients that go in this dish are:
 
### Salmon
 
You can fish for Salmon at Frosted Heights or at Sunlit Plateau.
 
### Rice
 
Rice is only available in the Glade of Trust. You can buy it at Goofy’s stall for 35 Star Coins.
 
### Sugarcane
 
To buy Sugarcane or Sugarcane Seeds for Teriyaki Salmon in Disney Dreamlight Valley, you will have to go to Goofy’s Stall at Dazzle Beach. You can buy Sugarcane Seed for 5 Star Coins or Sugarcane itself for 29 Star Coins after upgrading the stall.
 
### Soya
 
You can find Soya at Goofy’s stall in Sunlit Plateau. You can either buy Soya Seeds for 60 Star Coins or Soya itself for 104 Star Coins after upgrading the stall.
 
### Ginger
 
You can find Ginger at the Forgotten Lands. You will have to look for it and pick it up instead of buying it.
After acquiring the ingredients, go to Chez Remy and you can start preparing Teriyaki Salmon. Toss all the ingredients into the cooking pot and you are done. You can consume it to get 1726 Energy or Gift it to NPCs.
