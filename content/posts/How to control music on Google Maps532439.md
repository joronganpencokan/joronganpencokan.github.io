# Unlock the Ultimate Hack: Take Control of Your Music And Navigation with This Google Maps Trick!

Have you ever found yourself frantically switching between your music app and your navigation app while driving? Or have you ever missed a turn because your music was just too loud? Well, fear no more, for I have discovered the ultimate hack that will take control of your music and navigation simultaneously.

The hack involves using Google Maps, the popular navigation app, in a way that many people overlook. Did you know that Google Maps has a built-in music player? That's right – you can listen to your favorite tunes while getting directions to your destination.

Here's how to do it:

1. Open Google Maps and input your destination.

2. Once the directions have loaded, tap the three dots in the top right corner of the screen.

3. Select "Add stop" and input the name of a nearby business or landmark.

4. Tap "Directions" and choose a different route than the one suggested by Google Maps.

5. Once your new route is selected, tap the "Start" button.

6. While in navigation mode, swipe up from the bottom of the screen to reveal the music player.

7. Choose your preferred music app and start playing your favorite tunes.

Voila! You can now listen to music and receive turn-by-turn directions without having to constantly switch between apps. And the best part? You can customize your route to pass by your favorite coffee shop or fast food joint, making your journey even more enjoyable.

This hack is especially useful for long road trips, where you may find yourself getting bored or tired while driving. Switching up your music and route can make the journey more exciting and less monotonous.

So, next time you hit the road, remember to utilize Google Maps' built-in music player and take control of your music and navigation simultaneously. Safe travels, and happy listening!

## Control Music in Google Maps
 
This option is not enabled by default. You need to do it manually. Here’s how to do it.
 
First and foremost you need to make sure you have the latest version. Go to the Google Play Store and update it to the latest version if the app is outdated. Then, open Google Maps and look for the hamburger menu. It is the icon with 3 horizontal lines. You will find it at the top of the app. Tap on it and a navigation drawer will open. Scroll down till you see Settings. Tap on it.
 
Once you are inside, find Navigation and tap on it. You need to enable Show media playback controls option. Then,  find Music Controls option. Tap on it and choose what music service you want to use. you get to choose from Spotify and Google Music.
 
This will enable music playback while navigating. The music will pause every time the app needs to tell you which way to go. This way you get to enjoy good music while getting the right directions.
