Disney’s Dreamlight Valley is known for its enchanting scenery, thrilling rides, and delicious food. This mouth-watering delicacy is a secret Disney recipe that has been pleasing taste buds for years. Here is your chance to uncover the magical recipe and recreate it in your very own kitchen.

Ingredients:

- 1 cup of mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
- 1 banana
- 1 cup of freshly squeezed orange juice
- 1 cup of plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon of honey

Instructions:

Step 1: Start by washing the berries and slicing the banana into small pieces.

Step 2: In a blender, puree the berries and banana until smooth.

Step 3: Add the plain Greek yogurt, freshly squeezed orange juice, and honey to the blender. Blend until all the ingredients are mixed together.

Step 4: Pour the dreamy puree mixture into a bowl.

Step 5: Allow the mixture to chill in the fridge for an hour.

Step 6: Once chilled, remove the bowl from the fridge and serve.

Step 7: To add a bit more magic to the dish, you can garnish it with a slice of lemon, a sprig of mint, or whipped cream.
