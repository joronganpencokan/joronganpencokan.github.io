# Unleash the Magic with Disney Dreamlight Valley's Secret Yule Log Recipe – Get Ready to Impress Your Guests!

Are you looking to bring some holiday magic to your home this year? Do you want to surprise your loved ones with a delicious dessert that will leave them speechless? Look no further than Disney Dreamlight Valley's Secret Yule Log Recipe!

This recipe is a well-kept secret, passed down from generation to generation at Disney's magical land. Now, with the release of the recipe to the public, you can bring a piece of Disney magic to your holiday feast!

Ingredients:

For the cake:

- 1 cup cake flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- 4 large eggs, separated
- ¾ cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the filling:

- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ cup of your favorite jam

For the frosting:

- 4 oz unsalted butter, softened
- 4 oz cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 2 tbsp heavy cream
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Red and green food coloring

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F. Grease a 10-by-15-inch jelly roll pan and line it with parchment paper.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.

3. In a separate large bowl, beat the egg yolks, sugar, and vanilla extract on high speed until the mixture is light and fluffy.

4. In a third bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.

5. Add the flour mixture to the egg yolk mixture and mix until fully combined.

6. Fold in the egg whites until no white streaks remain.

7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth it out with a spatula.

8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.

9. While the cake is baking, prepare the filling by whipping the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract together until the mixture forms stiff peaks.

10. Once the cake is finished baking, immediately turn it out onto a clean kitchen towel or a sheet of parchment paper sprinkled with powdered sugar.

11. Remove the parchment paper from the top of the cake and spread jam over the cake.

12. Spoon the whipped cream filling over the jam and carefully roll the cake into a log shape.

13. Place the cake seam side down on a serving dish and chill in the refrigerator for 1-2 hours.

14. To make the frosting, beat together the butter and cream cheese until smooth.

15. Gradually add the powdered sugar, heavy cream, and vanilla extract and mix until fully combined.

16. Divide the frosting into three bowls and mix in red and green food coloring in two of them.

17. Spoon the plain frosting onto the chilled yule log cake, making sure to cover it completely.

18. Use a spatula to make bark-like grooves along the length of the cake.

19. Spoon the red and green frosting onto the cake in small round dots to resemble holly berries.

20. Chill the finished cake in the refrigerator until it’s time to serve.

## How to Make Yule Log in Disney Dreamlight Valley?
 

 
To Cook Yule Log, you need the following ingredients:
 
- Wheat x1
 - Cocoa Bean x1
 - Vanilla x1
 - Cherry x1

 
Add these ingredients to the cooking stove and use one coal ore to make a tasty Yule Log.
 
You can grow Wheat in your field by purchasing Wheat Seeds from the Goofy Stall in Peaceful Meadows for 1 Star Coin. Cocoa Bean is harvested from Glade of Trust or Sunlit Plateau Biome. If you haven’t unlocked these Biomes, you can access them by spending 5000 Dreamlight on Glade of Trust Biome or you can spend 7000 Dreamlight on Sunlit Plateau to unlock it. Vanilla is a flower found in Sunlit Plateau. They are easy to notice by their white flowers in a dark green plant. You can find Cherry from Sunlit Plateau or Frosted Heights Biome.
 
### What are the uses of the Yule Log?
 
Yule Log restores 2,150 energy, which is equivalent to the restoration of some 5-star recipes. Also, it gives 213 Star Coins on selling, which is a decent amount for a 4-star recipe. You can even gift it to your companion as their favorite item of the day to increase the friendship level and this will be the best use of the recipe.
