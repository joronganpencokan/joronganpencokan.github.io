## Revolutionize Your Messaging Game: Learn To Schedule Text Messages With Google Messages Like A Pro!

Are you tired of missing important messages because you weren't able to send them at the right time? Do you want to be able to send messages to loved ones on their special occasions, even when you're busy with work or other commitments? If you're looking for a way to revolutionize your messaging game, then you need to learn how to schedule text messages with Google Messages!

Google Messages is one of the most popular messaging apps available on Android devices. It offers a range of features that make messaging a breeze, including the ability to schedule text messages. The scheduling feature allows you to send messages at a specific time and date, so you don't have to worry about forgetting to send a message or missing an important occasion.

In this article, we'll show you how to schedule text messages with Google Messages like a pro!

### Step 1: Open Google Messages

The first step is to open the Google Messages app. If you haven't downloaded it already, you can find it in the Google Play Store.

### Step 2: Create a New Message

To schedule a text message, you need to create a new message. Tap the "New Message" icon in the bottom right-hand corner of the screen.

### Step 3: Enter the Recipient's Details

Enter the recipient's details in the "To" field. You can type in their name or phone number, and Google Messages will suggest contacts from your address book.

### Step 4: Type Your Message

Type your message as you normally would. You can add emojis, photos, and even voice memos to your message.

### Step 5: Schedule Your Message

When you're ready to schedule your message, tap and hold the Send button. A new menu will appear that says "Send Later." Tap this option.

### Step 6: Set the Date and Time

You can now set the date and time that you want your message to be sent. You can choose from one of the suggested options, such as "Later Today" or "Tomorrow Morning," or you can set a custom time and date.

### Step 7: Confirm Your Message

Once you've set the date and time, it's time to confirm your message. You'll see a preview of your message and the date and time that it will be sent. If everything looks good, tap "Send."

### Step 8: Relax and Enjoy!

You've now scheduled your text message like a pro! Now all you have to do is relax and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with knowing your message will be sent at the perfect time.

In conclusion, scheduling text messages with Google Messages is an easy and convenient way to stay on top of your messaging game.
