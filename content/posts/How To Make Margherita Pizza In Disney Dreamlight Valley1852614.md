Ingredients:

- 1 ½ cups of all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- 1 tablespoon of sugar
- 1 tablespoon of active dry yeast
- 1 cup of warm water
- Salt
- 1 cup of tomato sauce
- 2 cups of grated mozzarella cheese
- Fresh basil leaves

Directions:

1. Combine all-purpose flour, sugar, and salt in a large mixing bowl.

2. Mix the active dry yeast with warm water in a separate bowl, stir and set aside for 5 minutes.

3. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the flour mixture and knead the dough for 10-12 minutes, or until it becomes smooth.

4. Leave the dough to rest for 30 minutes, covered in a warm and moist place.

5. Preheat your oven to 450°F/200°C.

6. Roll the dough onto a flat, lightly oiled baking tray.

7. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a little space around the edges.

8. Sprinkle the grated mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce and garnish with fresh basil leaves.

9. Bake the Margherita Pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes or until the crust turns golden brown and the cheese melts.

10. Let it cool for a couple of minutes before slicing and serving.
