So let’s get started! Here’s what you’ll need: 

Ingredients:

- 1 and ½ cups all-purpose flour 
- 1 tsp baking powder 
- ½ tsp baking soda 
- ¼ tsp salt 
- 2 tsp cinnamon 
- 1 tsp allspice 
- 1 tsp nutmeg 
- 1 tsp ginger 
- ½ cup unsalted butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 2/3 cup pumpkin puree 
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ cup milk 

For the coating:

- 1 cup sugar 
- 2 tsp cinnamon 

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a muffin tin with paper liners.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg, and ginger. Set aside.

3. In a separate mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add in the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.

4. Add in the pumpkin puree and vanilla extract, and mix until well combined.

5. Gradually mix in the dry ingredients until just combined, then add the milk and stir until the batter is smooth.

6. Using a scoop or spoon, fill the muffin tins ¾ of the way full with batter.

7. Bake the pumpkin puffs for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.

8. While the pumpkin puffs are baking, combine the sugar and cinnamon in a shallow dish.

9. Remove the pumpkin puffs from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.

10. Roll each pumpkin puff in the cinnamon sugar mixture until evenly coated.

11. Serve and enjoy!

## How to Make Pumpkin Puffs in Dreamlight Valley
 

 
You can make Pumpkin Puffs in this game by cooking pumpkin, eggs, and cheese. You need these ingredients in these numbers:
 
- Cheese: 1
 - Egg: 1
 - Pumpkin: 1
 - Coal: 1 (For cooking them)

 
Here is how to make it:
 
- Take the above ingredients and go to a Kitchen.
 - Interact with the pot to start cooking.
 - Add a pumpkin, an egg, and cheese to the pot.
 - After you are done adding these ingredients to the pot, click on the Start Cooking button below it.
 - This will consume one coal and start preparing the dish.
 - After the animation, you will have your Pumpkin Puffs ready.

 
### How to Get these Ingredients
 
Two of the three ingredients are pretty easy to get and even the third one isn’t too hard.
 
- Cheese: You can buy cheese from Remy. 1 cheese costs 180 Star Coins.
 - Eggs: You can also buy eggs from Remy. Eggs cost 220 Star Coins.
 - Pumpkin: Pumpkin is slightly hard to get. You can buy Pumpkin seeds from Goofy at his stand on the Forgotten Lands. These seeds cost 275 Star Coins. Then you will have to plant these seeds and wait for the pumpkin to grow. These take 4 hours in real-time to grow

 
### How to Use Pumpkin Puffs in Dreamlight Valley
 
You can use Pumpkin Puffs mostly for selling. They are a good way to farm Star Coins as 1 Pumpkin Puff sells for 1489 Star Coins. Other than that you can also use this dish for consumption or for giving it to NPCs.
