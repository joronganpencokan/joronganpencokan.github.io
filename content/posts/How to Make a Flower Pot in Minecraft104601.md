Now it's time to start building your flower pot. To create the base, take your dirt blocks and lay them out in a square shape, leaving a 1-block gap in the center. Make sure the square is even on all sides, and that it is the size you want your flower pot to be.

Once you have your base, it's time to start building the walls of your flower pot. To do this, stack your dirt blocks along the outside edges of the base, going up at least five blocks high. Make sure the walls are straight and even, and that they are the same height all the way around.

Step 6: Adding Water

To create the perfect environment for your flowers to grow, you will need to add water to your flower pot. To do this, take your water bucket and fill the center of the flower pot with water. Make sure the water is evenly distributed, and that it covers the entire bottom of the bowl.

Step 7: Planting Your Flowers

Finally, it's time to add your flowers to your newly created flower pot. You can use any type of flower you like, and you can even mix and match different colors and varieties.
