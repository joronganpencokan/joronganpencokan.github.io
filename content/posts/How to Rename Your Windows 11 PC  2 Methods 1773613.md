# Unlock the Secret Techniques for Renaming Your Windows 11 PC in Just 2 Simple Steps!

Windows 11 has a lot of cool features and customization options, and one of them is the ability to rename your PC. Renaming your PC not only makes it easier to identify on a network, but it can also be a fun and personal touch to your computer.

In this article, we'll show you how to rename your Windows 11 PC in just 2 simple steps using the Hugo writing format.

## Step 1: Open the Settings app

The first step to renaming your Windows 11 PC is to open the Settings app. Here's how:

1. Press the Windows key + I on your keyboard to open Settings.

2. Click on "System" in the left-hand menu.

3. Click on "About" in the left-hand menu.

4. Click on "Rename this PC" under "Device specifications."

## Step 2: Enter your new PC name

Once you've opened the "Rename this PC" settings, you can now enter your new PC name. Here's how:

1. In the "Rename your PC" window, click on the "Computer name" field.

2. Enter your new PC name.

3. Click on "Next."

4. A "Restart your PC" pop-up will appear. Click on "Restart now" to apply your new PC name. 

And that's it! You have successfully renamed your Windows 11 PC in just 2 simple steps.

## List of 2 Methods to Rename Your Windows 11 PC


There are two ways to rename a Windows 11 PC. You can change your Windows 11 PC’s name via Settings or via Command Prompt. Both of the methods were easy to follow. Hence, in this article, we are going to share the two best methods to rename your Windows 11 PC. Let’s check out.

 
### 1. Using System Settings


Well, in this method, we are going to use the System Settings page to rename Windows 11 computers. Here’s what you need to do.
Step 1. First of all, click on the Start button and select ‘Settings‘.

Step 2. On the Settings page, click on the ‘System‘ option.

Step 3. On the right pane, scroll down and click on the ‘About‘ option.

Step 4. On the next page, click on the ‘Rename this PC’ option.

Step 5. On the next Window, enter the PC name and click on the ‘Next‘ button.

Step 6. Finally, click on the ‘Restart Now’ button to rename the Windows 11 PC.

That’s it! You are done. This is how you can rename your Windows 11 PC.

 
### 2. Rename Windows 11 PC via Command Prompt


In this method, we are going to use the Command Prompt utility to rename the Windows 11 computer. Here’s what you need to do.
Step 1. First of all, open Windows Search and type in ‘Command Prompt. Right-click on the CMD and select ‘Run as administrator’

Step 2. On the Command Prompt Window, enter the following command:
wmic computersystem where name="%computername%" call rename name="NewPCName"

Important: Replace the ‘NewPCName’ with the new name for your PC.
Step 3. Command Promot will display the success message. It should display ‘Method Execution Successful’.

That’s it! You are done. Now just restart your PC to apply the changes.
