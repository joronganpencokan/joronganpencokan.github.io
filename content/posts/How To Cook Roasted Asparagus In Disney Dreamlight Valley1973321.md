Luckily for us, Disney has revealed their secret recipe for this delectable dish, so now we can all enjoy it at home. So let's get started!

Ingredients:

- 1 pound of asparagus, trimmed
- 1 tablespoon of olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon of freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit. 
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. 
3. Spread the asparagus out on the baking sheet. 
4. Drizzle the olive oil over the asparagus, making sure to coat each piece evenly. 
5. Sprinkle the salt and pepper over the asparagus. 
6. Roast the asparagus for 12-15 minutes, or until they are tender and slightly browned. 
7. Remove the asparagus from the oven and sprinkle the Parmesan cheese over the top. 
8. Serve hot and enjoy!

That's it! The secret to Disney's mouth-watering roasted asparagus is in the simplicity of the recipe. With only a few ingredients and a bit of time in the oven, you can enjoy this delicious dish in the comfort of your own home.
