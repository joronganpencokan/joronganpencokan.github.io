It is the most popular dessert in the valley, loved by both locals and tourists alike. But what makes this cheesecake so special? Well, it is the secret recipe, passed down from generation to generation, that makes this cheesecake so mouth-watering and delicious. And today, I am going to reveal that secret recipe to you!

Ingredients:

- 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 24 oz cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 4 large eggs, room temperature
- 1/3 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.
2. Combine the graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup granulated sugar, and melted butter in a bowl. Mix well and press onto the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Place in the freezer to set for 10-15 minutes.
3. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese, 1 cup granulated sugar, and vanilla extract until creamy and smooth.
4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
5. Mix in the heavy cream, sour cream, powdered sugar, lemon juice, and salt. Beat on low speed until well combined.
6. Pour the cheesecake mixture into the prepared crust.
7. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until the edges are set but the center is still slightly jiggly.
8. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for another 10-15 minutes.
9. Remove from the oven and let it cool completely to room temperature.
10. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 6 hours or overnight.
11. Decorate with your favorite toppings before serving.

And there you have it, the secret recipe for the most mouthwatering cheesecake in Magical Disney Dreamlight Valley. ## How to Make Cheesecake in Disney Dreamlight Valley?
 

 
For a perfect recipe right ingredients are essential. Hence, to make Cheesecake a 4-star recipe, you will require 4 ingredients. These ingredients are:
 
- 1x Cheese
 - 1x Wheat
 - 1x Sugarcane
 - 1x Any Fruit

 
These ingredients are available in different Biomes or after completing certain quests. Hence you might need to unlock various Biomes and locations to collect the required ingredients.
 
### Where To Find Ingredients for Cheesecake?
 
- You can buy Cheese from Chez Remy Pantry for 180 Star Coins. Chez Remy Pantry can be accessed after unlocking Chez Remy Restaurant.
 - You can grow Wheat in Peaceful Meadows after purchasing Wheat Seeds from Goofy Stall at Peaceful Meadows for 1 Star Coins. Also, if you are in hurry to prepare the recipe you can buy Wheat from the same Goofy Stall for 3-Star Coins.
 - Sugarcane is available in the Goofy Stall of Dazzle Beach for 29 Star Coins. Also, you can buy Sugarcane seeds from the same Goofy Stall for 5-Star Coins to grow Sugarcane in Dazzle Beach.
 - Sugar Cane seeds take around 17 minutes to grow into Sugar Cane Crops.
 - If you haven’t Unlocked Dazzle Beach, you can unlock it by spending 1000 Dreamlight.
 - Since there’s no specific fruit necessary to cook Cheesecake, hence you can use any fruit found in the game.
 - Although there are no seeds available for fruits in the game you can forage them from the plants.
