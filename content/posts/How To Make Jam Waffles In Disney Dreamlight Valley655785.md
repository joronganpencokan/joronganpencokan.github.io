Now, you can recreate this beloved breakfast treat in the comfort of your own home with the following recipe:

## Ingredients:

- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 1/2 stick unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 cup your favorite jam

## Instructions:

1. Preheat your waffle iron to the desired temperature.

2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.

3. In a separate bowl, beat the milk, egg, and melted butter together until smooth.

4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.

5. Ladle the batter onto the hot waffle iron, making sure to spread the batter evenly.

6. Spoon a dollop of your favorite jam onto the center of the batter, and use a spoon or knife to swirl it around.

7. Close the waffle iron and cook until the waffles are golden brown and crispy.

8. Serve immediately, topped with extra jam and whipped cream if desired.

This recipe yields 4-5 large waffles, perfect for a family breakfast or a decadent solo indulgence.

### Fruit
 
To make Jam Waffles in Disney Dreamlight Valley, you will need a fruit. There are a number of fruits available in the game and you can choose any of them for this recipe. You will find them spread out throughout the biomes.
 
- Apples at Plaza in Forgotten Lands

### Wheat
 
To get wheat, you will have to grow it using Wheat Seeds. You can get wheat seeds at Goofy’s stall in Peaceful Meadows. Repair his stall for 500 Star Coins and then upgrade the stall with another 1000 Star Coins to get wheat seeds. It is the cheapest ingredient you can buy in the game costing only 1 Star Coin. Grow wheat by planting them in rich soil.
 
### Milk
 
You can buy milk from Chez Remy’s back shelf. Unlock Chez Remy first and complete his quest to gain access to his back shelf. You can buy milk there for 230 Star Coins.

Eggs are also available at Chez Remy’s back shelf for 220 Star Coins. These are all the ingredients needed to make Jam Waffles in Disney Dreamlight Valley.
