So without further ado, here's the secret recipe for Disney Dreamlight Valley's potato puffs:

Ingredients:

- 6 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 1 egg, lightly beaten
- 1 cup milk
- Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, and black pepper. Mix well until everything is evenly incorporated.

2. Add in the lightly beaten egg and mix again until the mixture is smooth.

3. Gradually add in the milk while continuing to mix. You want the mixture to be slightly thin, but not too runny.

4. Heat up the vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot until it's hot. You want the oil to be hot enough that a drop of the potato mixture sizzles and floats to the surface immediately.

5. Using a spoon, drop small spoonfuls of the potato mixture into the hot oil. Make sure not to overcrowd the pan, as the potato puffs will expand as they cook.

6. Fry the potato puffs until they're golden brown on all sides, about 2-3 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to remove them from the oil and let them drain on a paper towel.

7. Serve the potato puffs hot, with your favorite dipping sauce.
