Ingredients

- 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 cup mixed dried fruits (raisins, apricots, cranberries, and cherries)
- 1/2 cup candied fruit peel
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a 9-inch (23 cm) round cake pan with butter and flour.

2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar with an electric mixer until fluffy.

4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, making sure to mix well after each addition.

5. Add the vanilla extract and mix in.

6. Gradually fold in the flour mixture, alternating with the buttermilk, until everything is well combined.

7. Fold in the dried fruits, candied fruit peel, and chopped walnuts.

8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for about 70 to 75 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

9. Take the cake out of the oven and let it cool completely in the pan.

10. Once cooled, remove the cake from the pan and store in an airtight container.
