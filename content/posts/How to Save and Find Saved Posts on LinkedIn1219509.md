LinkedIn is a goldmine for professionals looking to network, share industry insights, and build their personal brand. From thought leadership articles to job postings and industry news updates, there's no shortage of valuable content on this social networking platform.

As you scroll through your LinkedIn feed, you come across a resourceful post that you'd like to read later. You’re aware of the capability to save the post, but you’re not sure how to retrieve it conveniently. Unfortunately, you forget the title, and it's lost somewhere in the depths of your LinkedIn profile. Sounds familiar?

Well, not to worry, in this article, you'll learn how to save and recover your valuable LinkedIn posts like a pro - so you never have to lose them again!

Step 1: Saving Your Posts on LinkedIn

Saving posts on LinkedIn is easy. All you have to do is click the three-dot icon located in the top right corner of the post you'd like to save. A dropdown menu will appear, select the "Save" option, and voila, the post is saved to your LinkedIn account. Alternatively, you can save the post by clicking on the "MORE" option located just below the post and selecting the "Save" option.

Step 2: Accessing Saved Posts on LinkedIn

Retrieving your saved LinkedIn posts is a bit trickier, but once you get the hang of it, it's a game-changer. To access your saved posts, click on the "Me" tab located at the top right corner of your LinkedIn profile. Next, click on "Saved" in the dropdown menu, and a list of all your saved posts will show up.

Step 3: Advanced Save Features

LinkedIn has advanced features that allow you to save more than just a post. You can use these features to keep track of articles, jobs, companies, and people you find interesting. Here's how:

Save an Article

When you come across a useful article on LinkedIn, you can save it for later. To do this, click on the three-dot icon located in the top right corner of the article, select the "Save" option, and the article will be saved to your LinkedIn account. To access your saved articles, follow the same process as you would with saved posts.

Save a Job

If you're job-hunting on LinkedIn, you can save job listings that interest you. To save a job, click on the "Save" icon located next to the job title. To access your saved jobs, click on the "Jobs" tab located at the top of your LinkedIn page, and select "saved jobs" from the dropdown menu.

Save a Company

If you're interested in tracking a specific company on LinkedIn, you can save it for future reference. To save a company, click on the "More" drop-down button on the company's LinkedIn page, select the "Save" option, and the company will be saved to your LinkedIn account. To access your saved companies, click on the "Work" tab located at the top of your LinkedIn page, and select "Companies" from the dropdown menu.

Save a Person

If you've come across a profile you'd like to refer back to later, you can save it to your LinkedIn account. Click on the three-dot icon located on the user's profile, select "Save to PDF" option, and save the PDF to your computer.

In conclusion, saving and retrieving posts on LinkedIn is an essential feature that can help you keep track of valuable content and stay organized. With this article, you now have the knowledge to save your posts, retrieve them with ease, and even use advanced LinkedIn saving features to achieve your professional goals. So, start saving your LinkedIn posts and make the most of this indispensable social media platform!
