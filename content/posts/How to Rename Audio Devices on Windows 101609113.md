# You Won't Believe How Easy It Is to Give Your Audio Devices Personalized and Fancy Names on Windows 10!

In Windows 10, it is incredibly easy to personalize the names of your audio devices. Whether you want to assign a personalized name to your headphone or microphone, the process is quite simple and can be done quickly.

The process of assigning a personalized name to your audio devices is not only useful for easy identification but can also make your audio setup appear more professional and high-end. Here's how to do it!

## Step-by-step Guide:

1. Open the Windows Settings app. You can either do this by searching for it in the search bar or by pressing the Windows key + I.

2. Click on the "Devices" option.

3. Select the "Bluetooth & other devices" tab.

4. Find the audio device that you want to rename and click on it.

5. Click on the "Device properties" option below the device's name.

6. Under the "General" tab, you will see an option that says "Device name." Click on it and rename it to whatever you like.

7. Click "OK" to save your changes.

8. Voila! You have successfully renamed your audio device.

## Tips for Personalizing Your Audio Device Names

- Keep it short and sweet: Opt for a name that is easy to remember and type.

- Use fancy names: Get creative and use names that make your audio setup sound cutting-edge and professional.

- Add numbers or icons: If you have multiple devices and want to add some order to your setup, consider adding numbers or icons to each name.

In conclusion, assigning a personalized name to your audio devices is an easy and practical way to elevate your audio experience. With Windows 10, you can make your audio setup appear professional and high-end in a matter of seconds. So, go on, give your devices a fancy name and enjoy your personalized audio setup!

## Steps to Rename Audio Devices on Windows 10


The option to rename the audio devices is buried deep under the Settings. So, if you want to rename Audio devices on Windows 10, you are reading the right article. Below, we have shared a detailed guide on how to rename audio devices.

 
### Rename Audio Output Devices on Windows 10


In this method, we will share a step-by-step guide on renaming audio output devices on Windows 10. First, follow some of the simple steps shared below.
1. First of all, click on the Windows Start button and select Settings.

2. On the Settings page, click on the System option.

3. On the left pane, click on the Sound option.

4. Select the output device you want to rename and click on the Device Properties on the right pane.

5. On the next page, enter the new Audio output device name and click on the Rename button.

That’s it! You are done. This is how you can rename the audio output device on Windows 10 PC.

 
### Rename Audio Input Devices on Windows 10


Just like the output devices, you can rename the Audio Input devices as well. Audio input means microphone. Here’s what you need to do.
1. First of all, click on the Windows Start button and select Settings.

2. On the Settings page, click on the System option.

3. On the left pane, click on the Sound option.

4. On the right pane, select the device you want to rename under the Input section and click on the Device Properties.

5. Enter the audio input device name and click on the Rename button on the next screen.

That’s it! You are done. This is how you can rename the Audio output & input device on Windows 10.
