# Get Ready to Master Chief your Way to Victory! Halo 5 Guardians Pre-Order with Three Epic Editions Available Now!

Gamers around the world are gearing up for the next installment in the Halo franchise, Halo 5: Guardians. With just a few months left before its release, the excitement is palpable. Microsoft has announced three different editions of the game for fans who are eager to get their hands on it before it hits the shelves on October 27th, 2015.

## Standard Edition

The standard edition of Halo 5: Guardians comes with the game itself and nothing else. It's perfect for those who are on a tight budget or those who don't need all the extra goodies that come with the collector's editions. The standard edition is priced at $59.99, and you can pre-order it now from major retailers.

## Limited Edition

For those who want a little extra with their game, Microsoft is offering a limited edition of Halo 5: Guardians. This edition comes with a few extra goodies that are sure to please any fan of the franchise. In addition to the game itself, the limited edition includes a steelbook case, a Warzone REQ Bundle (14 Premium Requisition packs), access to the Halo 5: Guardians live-action digital series, and several exclusive in-game items. This edition is priced at $99.99, and you can pre-order it now from major retailers.

## Collector's Edition

The ultimate edition for die-hard Halo fans is the collector's edition. This edition comes with all the features of the limited edition and more. In addition to the steelbook case, Warzone REQ Bundle, and access to the digital series, the collector's edition includes a commemorative statue of Spartan Locke and Master Chief, a Halo: The Fall of Reach animated series, and an exclusive in-game item. This edition is priced at $249.99 and is only available at select retailers.

## Conclusion

Pre-ordering any one of these three editions of Halo 5: Guardians is sure to give gamers the ultimate experience. With all of the extras included, choosing the right edition is a matter of personal preference. Whether you want all the bonus content or just the basic game, Halo 5: Guardians is sure to bring players hours of entertainment as they take on the role of the Master Chief and work to save the universe once again. Get ready to master Chief your way to victory!

While Microsoft begins testing the multiplayer beta version of Halo 5: Guardians on December 29th (12 pm Eastern Time), the company is announcing today that is now taking pre-orders for the first person shooter game coming to Xbox One, which will release sometime in fall 2015. All those interested have the choice of pre-ordering one of the three editions of Halo 5: Guardians, including the “Limited Collector’s Edition” for $249.99.
 
The Standard Edition has a price tag of $59.99, like most Xbox One games, and it includes the game, plus a Halo 5: Guardians exclusive poster (only available for those who pre-order).
 
The Limited Edition has a price tag of $99.99 and it includes all the features of the Standard Edition, plus digital content “to enhance Spartan combat and exclusive items wrapped in a uniquely designed steel book.”
 
The Limited Collector’s Edition cost $249.99 and it includes everything from the Limited Edition, plus additional content such as “a commemorative numbered statute designed by 343 Industries.” Microsoft will be sharing more details for this edition on a later date.
 
Everyone who got access to the Halo 5: Guardians multiplayer beta will have their unlocked content automatically carried over to the final version at launch.
 
The multiplayer beta test of Halo begins December 29th and will end on January 18th, and it’s available for anyone who purchase Halo: The Master Chief Collection.
