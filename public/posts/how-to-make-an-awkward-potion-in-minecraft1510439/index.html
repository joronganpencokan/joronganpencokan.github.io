You Won’t Believe What Happens When You Mix THESE Items in Minecraft - Crafting the Ultimate Awkward Potion!#

Do you ever wonder what would happen if you mixed some of the most awkward ingredients together in Minecraft? Well, you’re in luck because we have experimented and will share with you the recipe for the ultimate Awkward Potion.

First off, let’s just say that the Awkward Potion is a very interesting item in Minecraft. It’s an essential element when creating potions as it serves as the base for many of them. To make things more interesting, the Awkward Potion effect is not harmful, but it doesn’t really do anything useful either. In short, it’s an awkward potion.

But what makes it so awkward? The answer lies in the recipe.

To craft the Awkward Potion, you will need the following ingredients:

  • Nether Wart
  • Water Bottle

Seems like a straightforward recipe, right? But the real fun begins when you start experimenting with the potion. By including new items to the recipe, whole new dimensions of potion-making are revealed.

Let’s add a Fermented Spider Eye to the mix. What happens now? The Awkward Potion turns into the Mundane Potion. It doesn’t have any effect on its own, but adding different ingredients to it can make it more useful.

Now, let’s take it up a notch and add Redstone. What happens now? The Mundane Potion turns into the Potion of Weakness. That’s right, adding Redstone to the Awkward Potion can give you the ability to weaken your enemies.

And, if you’re feeling even more adventurous, add Gunpowder to the mix. This will give you the Splash Potion of Weakness – with the added benefit of affecting multiple enemies at once.

In conclusion, the Awkward Potion may seem like a useless item in Minecraft, but it’s a crucial ingredient to creating other potions. And, by adding different ingredients, you can create a variety of potions with varying effects.

So, next time you’re out exploring the Minecraft world, be sure to experiment with different potions and ingredients, and you may just stumble upon the ultimate potion!

The Awkward Potion in Minecraft doesn’t have any power on its own; however, if you combine it with other ingredients, you can brew a lot of useful potions.

Instructions in this article apply to Minecraft for all platforms including Windows, PS4, and Xbox One.

How to Make an Awkward Potion in Minecraft#

Follow these steps to collect the materials and craft an Awkward Potion in Minecraft.

  • Craft Blaze Powder with a Blaze Rod.
  • Make a Crafting Table with 4 wood planks. Any type of plank (Warped Planks, Crimson Planks, etc.) will do.
  • Place the Crafting Table on the ground and open it to bring up the 3X3 crafting grid.
  • Craft a Brewing Stand. Put 1 Blaze Rod in the middle of the top row, then put 3 Cobblestones in the middle row.
  • Place the Brewing Stand on the ground and interact with it to bring up the brewing menu.
  • Add 1 Blaze Powder to the box in the upper-left corner of the brewing menu to activate the Brewing Stand.
  • Put a Water Bottle in one of the three boxes at the bottom of the brewing menu.
  • Make up-to three potions at once by putting Water Bottles in the other bottom boxes.
  • Put 1 Nether Wart in the box at the top of the brewing menu.
  • Wait for the brewing process to complete. When the progress bar is full, your bottle will contain an Awkward Potion.
