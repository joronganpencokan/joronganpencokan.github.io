Unlock the Secret: Copy Your Instagram Profile Link with Just One Tap!#

Do you struggle with copying your Instagram profile link every time someone asks for it? Or perhaps you’re looking for ways to promote your Instagram page without having to type out the link manually? If yes, then we’ve got some great news for you!

The Traditional Method: How to Copy Your Instagram Profile Link Manually#

Before we dive into the one-tap method, let’s first take a look at the traditional way of copying your Instagram profile link.

  1. Open your Instagram app on your smartphone.
  2. Go to your profile by tapping on the profile icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap on the three horizontal lines at the top right corner of the screen.
  4. Select “Settings” from the pop-up menu.
  5. Scroll down to the “Account” section and tap on “Public Access.”
  6. Tap on “Profile” and copy the link that appears under “Share Your Profile.”

As you can see, the traditional method involves quite a few steps, which can be time-consuming and tedious. But don’t worry, there’s a better way!

The One-Tap Method: How to Copy Your Instagram Profile Link in Just One Tap#

Instagram has a lesser-known feature that allows you to copy your profile link with just one tap. Here’s how it works:

  1. Open your Instagram app on your smartphone.
  2. Go to your profile by tapping on the profile icon at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Tap and hold on your profile picture.
  4. A pop-up menu will appear. Select “Copy Profile URL” from the options.
  5. The link to your Instagram profile will now be copied to your clipboard.

That’s it - your profile link is now ready to be pasted anywhere you want. Whether you’re sharing it on other social media platforms or simply sending it to a friend, you can now do so with ease.

How to Copy Your Own Instagram Profile Link?#

To know how to copy your Instagram profile link from App, just follow these steps:

  1. Launch the Instagram application on your Android and iOS devices.

  2. Tap on the Profile tab from the bottom-right corner of the screen.

  3. Tap on the hamburger icon from the top-right corner of the screen.

  4. Tap on the QR code option.

  5. Tap on the Share icon from the top right corner of the QR code screen.

  6. Tap on the note-taking app on your phone. We have chosen the Keep Notes app for demonstration purposes, as shown below.

  7. A note preview will be shown on the screen. Swipe up the QR image to reveal the profile link below.

  8. Double-tap on the profile link to select it and tap on Copy.

  9. Paste the link wherever you want to share it.

This is how to copy your Instagram profile link from app.

How to Copy Your Instagram Profile Link Using Your Instagram Username?#

To know how to copy your Instagram profile link from app using your Instagram username, follow these steps:

  3. Tap on the Username field.

  4. Double-tap on the username and tap on the Copy option to copy it.

  5. Then, paste the copied username into the search bar of your browser after the instagram.com/ link. Like this:

instagram.com/username

  1. Double-tap on this newly created link to select it and tap on Copy to share it with others.

This is how to copy your own Instagram profile link from app using your Instagram username.

How to Copy Your Instagram Profile Link Using a PC or a Computer?#

You can learn how to copy your Instagram profile link using a PC or a Computer with the help of the following steps.

  1. Visit the Instagram website on your browser.

  2. Enter your email/phone number/username and password and then click on the Log in option.

  3. Click on your profile username to go to your profile page.

  4. Click on the address bar from the top of the browser to select the profile link.

  5. Then, press Ctrl + C (for Windows)/Cmd + C (for Mac) to copy your profile link.

This is how you can copy your Instagram profile link using a PC or a computer.

How to Copy Your Instagram Profile Link Using Your Other Instagram Account?#

How Do I Copy My Instagram Link to My Bio on Facebook?#

To know how do I copy my Instagram link to my bio on Facebook, just follow the below steps:

Note: Make sure you are logged into the Facebook and Instagram app.
