Setting Up Google Drive#

The first step is to set up Google Drive on your iPhone. To do this, open the Google Drive app and sign in with your Google account. Once you’ve signed in, you’ll be able to access your Google Drive from your iPhone.

Backing Up WhatsApp on iPhone to Google Drive#

Once you’ve set up Google Drive, you can now back up your WhatsApp data to Google Drive. To do this, open the WhatsApp app on your iPhone and tap on “Settings.” Then, tap on “Chats.” Next, tap on “Chat Backup.” Finally, tap on “Back Up Now” to begin the backup process.

Restoring WhatsApp from Google Drive#

If you ever need to restore your WhatsApp data from Google Drive, you can do so by opening the WhatsApp app on your iPhone and tapping on “Settings.” Then, tap on “Chats.” Next, tap on “Chat Backup.” Finally, tap on “Restore Backup” to begin the restore process.
