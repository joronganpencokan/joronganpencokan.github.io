Definition of GERD#

GERD is a chronic digestive disorder that occurs when stomach acid or bile flows back into the esophagus. This can cause a burning sensation in the chest, known as heartburn, as well as other symptoms such as regurgitation and difficulty swallowing. GERD can be caused by a variety of factors, including diet, lifestyle, and medications.
