Introduction#

Are you tired of seeing your Facebook page show up in your personal account? Do you want to remove it from your personal account, but don’t know where to start? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! This amazing trick will show you how to instantly remove your Facebook page from your personal account.

Overview of the Trick#

The trick is incredibly simple and only requires a few steps. All you have to do is log into your Facebook account, select your page, select settings, and select remove page. That’s it! Once you complete these steps, your Facebook page will be removed from your personal account.

Benefits of Removing Your Facebook Page from Your Personal Account#

Removing your Facebook page from your personal account has many benefits. First, it will help you keep your personal and business accounts separate. This will make it easier for you to manage both accounts. Additionally, it will help you keep your personal information private. By removing your page from your personal account, you can ensure that your personal information is not visible to anyone who visits your page.

Steps to Remove Your Facebook Page from Your Personal Account#

Now that you know the benefits of removing your Facebook page from your personal account, let’s take a look at the steps you need to take to do it.

Log into Your Facebook Account#

The first step to removing your Facebook page from your personal account is to log into your Facebook account. To do this, simply enter your username and password on the Facebook homepage. Once you’re logged in, you’ll be taken to your profile page.

Select Your Page#

Once you’re logged into your account, the next step is to select your page. To do this, click on the “Pages” tab on the left side of your profile page. This will take you to a list of all the pages you have created. Select the page you want to remove from your personal account.

Select Settings#

Once you’ve selected your page, the next step is to select “Settings”. To do this, click on the “Settings” tab at the top of the page. This will take you to the page settings page.

Select Remove Page#

The final step is to select “Remove Page”. To do this, click on the “Remove Page” button at the bottom of the page. Once you click on this button, your page will be removed from your personal account.
