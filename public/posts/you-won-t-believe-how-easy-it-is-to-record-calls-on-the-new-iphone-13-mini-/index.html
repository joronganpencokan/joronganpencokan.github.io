Step-by-Step Guide to Recording Calls on the iPhone 13 Mini#

Now that you know the benefits of recording calls on the iPhone 13 Mini, let’s look at how to activate and use the call recording feature.

Activating the Call Recording Feature#

The first step is to activate the call recording feature. To do this, open the Settings app on your iPhone 13 Mini and tap on ‘Phone’. Then, tap on ‘Call Recording’ and toggle the switch to ‘On’.

Recording the Call#

Once the call recording feature is activated, you can start recording calls. To do this, simply tap the ‘Record’ button that appears on the screen during a call.

Saving and Sharing the Recording#

Once the call is finished, the recording will be saved to your phone. You can then access it in the ‘Recents’ tab of the Phone app. From here, you can share the recording with others via text message, email, or any other messaging platform.
