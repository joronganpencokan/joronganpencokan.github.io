Unlimited Money

Money is the most valuable commodity in GTA 5. It can buy you weapons, properties, and vehicles, and can help you complete missions. One of the best cheat codes to get unlimited money is “HESOYAM”. This code will instantly give you $250,000 in-game cash, which you can use to buy whatever you need.

God Mode

God mode is one of the most popular cheat codes in GTA 5. With this cheat, you become invincible, which means that you can’t take any damage. To activate god mode, enter the cheat code “PAINKILLER.” Once activated, you can become nearly invincible for as long as you want.

Increasing and Decreasing Wanted Level

The cops are always on the lookout for criminals in GTA 5, and once you commit a crime, they’ll chase after you. However, you can use cheat codes to increase or decrease your wanted level. “LAWYERUP” is the cheat code to decrease your wanted level while “FUGITIVE” is the code to increase your wanted level.

Unlimited Weapons

Are you ever stuck in a mission where you have run out of ammo, and you desperately need more weapons? Well, fear not, as you can unlock unlimited weapons in GTA 5 using cheat codes. “FULLCLIP” is the cheat code that gives you unlimited ammunition for all your weapons. You can also use “PROFESSIONALTOOLS” to get all the weapons in the game.
