Disney’s Secret Recipe Revealed: Learn How To Make The Most Delicious Pumpkin Soup In Dreamlight Valley!

If you’re a fan of Disney, then you know that their food is just as magical as their movies. From Mickey Mouse-shaped treats to Dole Whip, Disney has a way of making food taste even better. But what if we told you that you could recreate a Disney recipe at home? And not just any recipe, but the most delicious pumpkin soup from Dreamlight Valley?

Yes, you read that right. Disney’s secret recipe for pumpkin soup has been revealed, and we’re here to share it with you. So grab your apron, stock up on ingredients, and let’s get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups canned pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie filling)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, whisk together the pumpkin puree, heavy cream, and chicken broth over medium heat.
  2. Add in the brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, and nutmeg. Whisk until everything is combined.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Taste the soup and add salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Serve hot and garnish with croutons or roasted pumpkin seeds, if desired.

This pumpkin soup recipe is perfect for chilly fall evenings or if you’re feeling a bit under the weather. The creaminess of the soup combined with the spices creates a warm, comforting feeling in your belly. And since it’s Disney’s recipe, you know it’s going to be extra special.

The pumpkin soup recipe is actually from the Storyteller’s Café at Disney’s Grand Californian Hotel & Spa. It’s a go-to for many guests during the fall season, and now you can enjoy it in the comfort of your own home.
