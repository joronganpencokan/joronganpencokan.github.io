Screen recording is easy to do on the iPhone 12 Mini. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

To start screen recording, open the Control Center by swiping down from the top right corner of the screen.

Tap the Screen Recording Icon#

Select the Microphone Option#

Once you tap the Screen Recording icon, a new window will appear. Here, you can select the microphone option to record audio along with your screen recording.

Start and Stop Recording#
